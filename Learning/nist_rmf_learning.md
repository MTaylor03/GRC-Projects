# Notes From NIST RMF Overview

## Overview:
### 1. Prepare
- Goal is to ensure readiness to manage risk
- Organization assigns roles, strategies, controls, and assessments
- Important to implement CI/CD to stay up to date
### 2. Categorize
- Determine impact based on CIA triad
- Document the categorizations and approve them
### 3. Select
- All about choosing the controls needed to protect the organization
- Define baselines to be gap analyzed later on
- Plans that reflect the reasoning for the control
### 4. Implement
- Implement the selected controls
- Update security plans and policies to include the controls added
### 5. Assess
- Here is the gap analysis step
- Look at all the controsl and match their implementation to the defined baselines
- Remediate as needed
### 6. Authorize
- Risk owner looks at the risk to see if its below the organizations risk threshold
- Approve or deny the controls
### 7. Monitor
- This is where we use the continuous monitoring strategies put in place
- Analyze what we learn about the monitoring
